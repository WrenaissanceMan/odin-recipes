Tacos

Description

These tacos are a simple, healthy twist on the popular Mexican dish.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork mince
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 can mixed beans, drained
  • 1 sachet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 12 taco shells

Instructions

  1. Heat some oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat, then cook onion until translucent.
  2. Add mince, then cook until browned, breaking it up as you go.
  3. Reduce heat, add seasoning and water, then stir thoroughly.
  4. Add carrots and beans, mixing everything together well.
  5. Cook until sauce thickens, then serve in taco shells.
