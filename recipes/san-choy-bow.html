Ingredients

San Choy Bow

  • Baby Gem lettuce
  • 500g chicken mince
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 tins water chestnuts, drained & finely chopped
  • 1 tsp minced ginger

Sauce

  • 3 tsp cornflour
  • 6 tbsp water
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp rice wine vinegar
